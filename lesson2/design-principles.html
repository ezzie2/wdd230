Contrast


Netflix

netflix.com

Their design is pretty simple, but it provides a quick glimpse to their services. By using the black background they are able to point the user to what is important, like signing up with an email address. They area able to point out their services as well right away since the font is differnt sizes.

White Space & Clean Design

Prezi

prezi.com

The white space contained in this site provides focus. One is able to be guided to that which is important without having to be familiar with the site. Also, it is easy to follow along without been confused at the content given.

Hick's Law


Cafe Rio

caferio.com

The Hick's Law states that by having less one is able to make a choice. Cafe Rio's site does use this principle. As one makes a choice, it takes you to another section where you are given options. Some have more than others, but one is not innudiated with too many choices. The filters they use can be the type of food you want (Entree, Dessert, Appetizer, etc.).
