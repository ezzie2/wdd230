Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide resources to both residents and potential residents. The site will have county information such as local schools, businessess, and city-wide events. Visitors will be able to see a gallery of photos, which showcases the cities within Davis County. Also, they will have a page where businesses receive a weekly spotlight.
